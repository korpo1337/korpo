# KORPO v2 — Cost Estimate

## Smart Contract Deployment
| Item | Cost (USD) |
|------|-----------|
| Contract deployment on Base | ~$1-$4 |
| Initial verification on Basescan | Free |

## Ongoing Costs
| Item | Cost per action |
|------|----------------|
| Claim (per wallet per day) | ~$0.06-$0.30 |
| Transfer (no burn) | ~$0.01-$0.07 |
| Transfer (with burn) | ~$0.02-$0.10 |
| Timelock queue | ~$0.01-$0.04 |
| Timelock execute | ~$0.01-$0.04 |

## Total Budget Required
- **Minimum for deployment**: 0.005 ETH (~$12.50)
- **Comfortable buffer**: 0.01 ETH (~$25)
- **No ongoing costs** — users pay their own gas for claims

## Revenue Model
**None.** This is a pure UBI experiment. No team allocation, no ICO, no fees. The token's value comes entirely from community adoption and usage.
