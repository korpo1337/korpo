# KORPO v2 — Project Summary

## What is KORPO?
KORPO is a minimal UBI (Universal Basic Income) experiment token on Base. One sentence: **Everyone can claim KORPO for free, daily, and every transfer above 100 KORPO burns 0.5% — creating natural scarcity from usage.**

## Why does it exist?
To prove that a token can be distributed fairly, without ICO, without team allocation, without insider advantage. Pure participation-based distribution.

## Key Parameters
- **Name**: KORPO
- **Symbol**: KORPO
- **Chain**: Base (testnet: Base Sepolia)
- **Total Supply**: 1,000,000,000 KORPO (1 billion)
- **Daily Claim**: 100 KORPO per wallet
- **Claim Cooldown**: 24 hours
- **Burn**: 0.5% per transfer > 100 KORPO
- **Admin Timelock**: 24 hours for all admin actions
- **Contract**: 1 file, ~165 lines of Solidity

## What it does NOT do
- No ICO, no presale, no fundraising
- No staking, no yield, no "passive income"
- No referral system, no multi-level
- No governance (immutable constants)
- No upgradeability
