# KORPO — Universal Basic Income on Base | Gauge Application

## 1. Project Overview

**KORPO** is a Universal Basic Income (UBI) token native to Base, designed to make decentralized, permissionless income accessible to anyone with an Ethereum wallet. Every eligible wallet can claim **100 KORPO per day** — no staking, no gating, no capital required. KORPO embodies the belief that financial sovereignty should begin with free, fair access.

The concept is simple yet powerful: **every human deserves a baseline of economic participation**, and the blockchain is the perfect delivery mechanism. KORPO turns that belief into a live, on-chain system.

### Deflationary by Design

Unlike most tokens that inflate to reward participants, KORPO operates a **reverse model**: the supply is fixed (300 KORPO), and every single transfer reduces it by 0.5%. This means:

- **Scarcity increases with adoption** — the more people use KORPO, the rarer it becomes
- **No inflationary pressure** — no vesting cliffs, no team unlocks, no hidden emissions
- **Built-in value accrual** — holders benefit from the burn of every other holder's transactions

The daily claim distributes tokens freely, while the burn ensures the circulating supply is always shrinking relative to activity.
